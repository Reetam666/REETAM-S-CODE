Welcome to Our Beautiful Village Bhusuk

Bhusuk, a picturesque village in East Sikkim, offers a tranquil escape amidst the stunning natural beauty of the Himalayas. Located approximately 17 km from Gangtok, the capital city of Sikkim, Bhusuk is a perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful getaway.
